About Uzima VM Portal

The Uzima VM Control Portal provides a secure, role-based interface for managing research and data workloads in the Uzima Data Science Lab, in collaboration with Aga Khan University Hospital. VM lifecycle actions are coordinated to optimize resources and maintain stability.
